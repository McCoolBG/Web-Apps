Tower Advanced 14

  Coordination:
  Ground Control Request:
  Crossing:
		Ground: Local, Ground, cross Runway Two One Right at Hotel.
  Proceed On:
		Ground: Local, Ground, proceed on Runway Two One Right at Hotel, for runway check.
		Ground: Local, Ground, proceed on Runway Two One Left at Hotel, for runway check.
  
 
   Local Control Response to Request:
   Crossing: 
		 Local: Cross Runway Two One Right at Hotel.
   Proceed On:
		 Local: Proceed on Runway Two One Right at Hotel.
		 Local: Proceed on Runway Two One Left at Hotel.
 
 
   Instructions Passed to Vehicles:
   Crossing:
		 Ground: Airfield One, cross Runway Two One Right at Hotel, report when off.
   Proceed On:
		 Ground: Airfield Three, proceed on Runway Two One Right at Hotel, report when off.
		 Ground: Airfield Two, proceed on Runway Two One Left at Hotel, report when off.

 
    Vehicles reports off runway or crossing complete:
	Crossing:
		Ground: Local, ground, crossing complete, Runway Two One Right at Hotel.
	Proceed On:
		Ground: Local, ground, all vehicles off Runway Two One Right, Runway Two One Right is your control.
		Ground: Local, ground, all vehicles off Runway Two One Left, Runway Two One Left is your control.

 
    Restricted Low Approaches:

	Coordination between ground and local:
		Local: Ground, local, a C-130 will conduct a restricted low approach over Runway Two One Right.
		Local: Ground, local, a A-10 will conduct a restricted low approach over Runway Two One Left.
 
 
 Local to Aircraft:
		Local: Hurky Three Five, check wheels down, Runway Two One Left, wind estimated two three zero at 6, cleared low approach  
        at or above two thousand two hundred, traffic vehicles on runway.
        
 
		Local: Storm Five Two, check wheels down, Runway Two One Right, wind estimated two three zero at seven, cleared low approach at or
         above two thousand two hundred, traffic vehicle on runway.
  
 
Ground to vehicle:
		Ground: Airfield One, a C-130 will conduct a low approach five hundred feet over Runway Two One Left.
		Ground: Airfield Three, a A-10 will conduct a low approach five hundred feet over Runway Two One Right.
