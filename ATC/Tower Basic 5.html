Emergency Tower

Tower Basic 5

Control Overhead Pattern:

	Non-Based Assigned Aircraft
		With Current ATIS:
			Local: Raptor Eight Eight, pattern altitude three thousand five hundred, right turns, report initial.
            		
     
		Doesn’t mention ATIS:
			Local: Raptor Eight Eight, verify you have information Alpha, pattern altitude three thousand five hundred, right turns, report initial.
            		
           
		Report the wrong ATIS:
			Local: Raptor Eight Eight, information Alpha now current, Runway two One Right, wind calm, altimeter three zero zero one, 
			       pattern altitude three thousand five hundred, right turns, report initial.
                   	
           
                   
	Based Assigned Aircraft:
    
		With Current ATIS:
			Local: Storm Five Two, report initial.
                     
		Doesn’t mention ATIS:
			Local: Storm Five Two, verify you have information Alpha, report initial.	
            
		Report the wrong ATIS:
			Local: Storm Five Two, information Charlie now current, wind calm, altimeter two niner niner two, report initial.
            	
           
            
	Spacing Maneuvers:
    
		Report Break:
        
			Without Sequence:
				Local: Eagle Two Eight, report break.
                
			With Sequence:
				Local: Raptor Eight Eight, number two follow C-130 short final, report break.
                	
     
                
		Break at Midfield:
        
			With Sequence:
				Local: Falcon Six One, break at midfield, number two follow C-130 base, report break.
                     
		Break at Departure End:
        
			With Sequence:
				Local: Blade Four Four, break at departure end, number two follow heavy C-5 base, report break.
                		
     

Protect the Overhead:

	Departure:
    
		Without traffic:
			Local: Raptor Eight Four, Runway Two One Right, wind calm, cleared for takeoff, maintain at or below three thousand until departure end.
            			
     
            
		With Traffic:
			Local: Raptor Eight Five, Runway Two One Right, wind two three zero at five, cleared for takeoff, 
            	               maintain at or below three thousand until departure end, traffic F-16 break.
                    		
     
            
	Go-around:
    
		Pilot Initiated:
			Local: Raptor Eight Eight, maintain at or below three thousand until departure end.
            
		Controller Initiated:
			Local: Raptor Eight Five, go-around aircraft on runway, maintain at or below three thousand until departure end.
